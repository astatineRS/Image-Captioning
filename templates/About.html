Welcome to

FORESTY

FORESTY is an image captioning model. Image captioning models are currently owned by some big companies such as Instagram, Facebook and Google.


FORESTY is different from such models as we want our model to be publically available. Inspired by the open source models like stable diffusion and auto gpt, we want FORESTY to be an open source library to revolutionise the image captioning community.
